About

It started as a spreadsheet.

After the Grubhub layoffs, I tried to help a few former teammates land on their feet. Names turned into rows. Rows turned into patterns. And pretty quickly, it was clear this wasn’t just a list.

It was a broken system.

Hundreds of talented people all hitting “apply” at the same time. Recruiters overwhelmed with noise. Great candidates getting lost somewhere between submission #1 and #2,500.

So instead of contributing to the noise, I tried something different.

I started organizing signal.

What began as a Google Sheet tracking ~300 impacted employees evolved into a searchable directory designed to do one thing well:

Make it easier to find great people, faster.

Not by volume. By clarity.

What This Is

This isn’t a job board.

It’s a curated, opt-in network of operators across engineering, product, data, go-to-market, and more — people who have already been vetted by experience, not keywords.

  • Built real systems
  • Solved real problems
  • Now happen to be in the market at the same time

How It Works

  • Profiles are submitted voluntarily
  • Each submission is reviewed before publishing
  • Only publicly shareable info (e.g., LinkedIn) is included
  • Profiles can be updated or removed at any time

Simple by design. Intentional by default.
