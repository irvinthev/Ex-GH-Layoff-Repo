About This Project

This started as a simple Google Sheet tracking ~300 individuals navigating layoffs.

It evolved into a searchable directory to reduce noise and increase signal — helping recruiters quickly find high-quality talent, and helping impacted individuals get visibility.

Why It Exists

  • Layoffs create chaos — good people get lost in volume
  • Traditional job boards favor applications, not connections
  • We wanted to make talent discoverable, not buried

How It Works

  • Profiles are opt-in via Google Form
  • Data is reviewed before publishing
  • Directory is updated regularly

About Me

Built by an internal audit / risk leader who got tired of watching broken systems fail people during layoffs — and decided to build something better.
